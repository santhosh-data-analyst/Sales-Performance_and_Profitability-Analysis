# Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (Power BI)

## Business Problem
Sales growth does not always translate into profitability. This project identifies
profit leakage driven by unprofitable products, aggressive discounting, and customer behavior.

## Objectives
- Identify loss-making products and customers
- Quantify profit leakage and revenue at risk
- Provide actionable recommendations (Drop, Renegotiate, Monitor, Protect)

## Dataset
- Orders, Products, Customers, Discounts
- Time period: 7 days
- Granularity: Transaction-level, aggregated to Product and Customer levels

## Dashboard Pages
### Page 1 – Sales Performance Overview
- Revenue, Profit, Margin trends
- Regional and category performance

### Page 2 – Product Profitability Analysis
- Product-level profitability landscape
- Identification of structurally unprofitable products

### Page 3 – Customer & Discount Profitability Analysis
- Discount vs Profit Margin scatter
- Profit leakage zones
- Customer action framework

## Key Insights
- High discounts strongly correlate with negative profit margins
- Several high-revenue products destroy value
- A significant portion of revenue is exposed to discount-driven profit leakage

## Business Impact
- Enables pricing discipline
- Supports product rationalization
- Highlights customers requiring immediate intervention
